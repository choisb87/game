# Bubble Pop - Game Plan

## Concept
Colorful bubbles rise from the bottom of the screen. Tap them to pop and score points. Miss too many and it's game over. Bubbles speed up as your score increases.

## Core Mechanics
- Bubbles spawn at random X positions at the bottom
- Each bubble floats upward at varying speeds
- Tap a bubble to pop it (+10 points)
- If a bubble escapes the top, lose a life (3 lives total)
- Speed and spawn rate increase every 50 points
- Combo system: pop bubbles quickly for bonus points

## Monetization Ideas
- Rewarded video ads to continue after game over
- Interstitial ads between rounds (every 3rd game)
- IAP: cosmetic bubble skins and pop effects
- Daily challenge mode (premium unlock)
